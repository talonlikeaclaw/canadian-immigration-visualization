### Response Delay

- We are expected to respond to any queries within 24 hours.
- If anyone is unable to finish a task in time or they will be busy or potentially unable to work on the project, they should communicate with their teammates as soon as possible. Communication is key.
    - We will communicate through our discord server in these cases.

## Decision Making

- Any new or non-required features have to be approved by the other teammates during the meetings before the issue is created.
- We will create issues on GitLab for any tasks that need to be done and let the other teammates know by sending the issue link.
- Ary dicussions on an issue should take place in the comments of the issue.

## Merge Request Expectations

- No giant merge requests (one issue par MR).
- A MR should be associated with an issue. 
    - Create the MR from the issue on GitLab.
- A MR should be approved by at least one team member.
    - The reviewer should thoroughly check through the MR.
- The MR should pass the `.gitlab-ci.yml` pipeline.
